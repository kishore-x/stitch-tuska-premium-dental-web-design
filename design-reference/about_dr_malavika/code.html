Meet Dr. Malavika

12+ Years

Clinical Excellence

format_quote

"Children deserve more than treatment for dental problems; they deserve care that identifies and addresses the underlying causes..."

— Dr. Malavika

Dr. Malavika is a pediatric dentist dedicated to providing comprehensive, child-centered oral healthcare from birth to 20 years of age. Her philosophy is simple—children deserve more than treatment for dental problems; they deserve care that identifies and addresses the underlying causes, helping them achieve healthy and functional oral development and lifelong confident smiles.

Her journey began at Vinayaka Mission's Sankarachariyar Dental College, where she developed her passion for clinical excellence. Her approach integrates the latest clinical advancements with a profound understanding of child psychology and growth patterns.

With over 12 years of clinical excellence, she works closely with parents to educate them on nutritional counseling, oral hygiene practices, and myofunctional habits to ensure the best possible outcomes for her young patients. By focusing on preventive strategies and early intervention, she helps families build a foundation for permanent wellness.
